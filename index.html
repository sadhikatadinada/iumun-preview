Letter
From The
Secretary General

Dear Delegates, Advisors, and Esteemed Guests,

I am delighted to welcome you to the prestigious International United Model United Nations 2023. As we embark on this remarkable journey, I am reminded of the profound words of Cicero, "Res publica res populi" - "The state is the concern of the people." At IUMUN 2023, we are dedicated to embodying this principle by fostering a diverse, dynamic, and global community of young leaders committed to fruitful debate, negotiations, and resolutions.

Our committees, each carefully curated to mirror the complexity of the world we inhabit, serve as arenas for the exchange of ideas, collaboration, and diplomacy. We believe that in unity, there is strength, and through your diverse representations and perspectives, we hope to find innovative solutions to the pressing challenges our world faces today. In the words of Seneca, "Sapientia non quaeritur in lapidibus" - "Wisdom is not to be found in stones." It is to be found in the minds and hearts of those who gather here to engage in thoughtful discourse and forge bonds of understanding.

IUMUN 2023 promises to be an unforgettable journey filled with challenges, growth, and the opportunity to make a lasting impact. Our Secretariat, committed to excellence, is here to ensure that you have an enriching experience. I invite you to seize this opportunity, for as Cicero wisely said, "Fortuna est caeca" - "Fortune is blind." Let us navigate the uncertain seas of diplomacy with courage, intellect, and empathy
